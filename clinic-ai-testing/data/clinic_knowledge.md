## 3. Accepted Insurance Providers

We accept the following in-network insurance plans:

- Blue Cross Blue Shield (all PPO and HMO plans)
- Aetna (PPO, HMO, and Medicare Advantage)
- Cigna (Open Access Plus and LocalPlus)
- UnitedHealthcare (Choice Plus only — please confirm at booking)

Please bring your insurance card and a valid photo ID to every visit.

## 4. Insurance Providers We Do NOT Accept

The following providers are **not** in our network and will be billed as
out-of-pocket:

- Kaiser Permanente (any plan)
- Tricare (any branch)
- Medicaid Managed Care (we accept traditional Medicaid only)

Patients covered by these plans are welcome as self-pay. Please request a
good-faith estimate before the visit.

## 6. Appointment Booking Process

Patients can book in three ways:

1. **Online patient portal:** https://portal.sunrisefamilyclinic.example
   — available 24/7, recommended for routine visits.
2. **Phone:** (217) 555-0155, staffed Monday–Friday 8:00 AM – 5:30 PM.
3. **Walk-in:** Same-day urgent visits are accepted between 8:00 AM and
   11:00 AM on weekdays, subject to provider availability. Walk-ins are NOT
   offered on Saturdays.

All new patients must complete the intake form before the first visit.

## 7. Cancellation Policy

- Appointments may be cancelled or rescheduled **up to 24 hours** before the
  scheduled time at no charge.
- Cancellations made **less than 24 hours in advance** incur a $35 late-cancel
  fee.
- **No-shows** are charged $50. Three no-shows within 12 months may result in
  discharge from the practice.
- The fee is waived in documented emergencies (illness, hospitalisation,
  family bereavement).

## 8. Medication Refill Policy

- Requests for refills must be submitted through the patient portal or by
  calling the nurse line at (217) 555-0160.
- Standard refill requests are processed within **2 business days**.
- Controlled substances (e.g. Schedule II medications) require an in-person
  visit and **cannot be refilled by phone**.
- Before changing any dose, stopping a medication, or combining medications,
  **please consult your doctor.** Do not self-adjust prescriptions based on
  information found online or shared by other patients.

## 9. Emergency Contact and After-Hours Instructions

- For life-threatening emergencies, **call 911 immediately** or go to the
  nearest emergency room.
- For urgent but non-life-threatening issues outside of business hours, call
  our 24/7 nurse triage line at **(217) 555-0199**.
- For non-urgent questions, send a message through the patient portal; staff
  will respond on the next business day.

## 10. Billing and Payment

- We accept cash, major credit cards, and HSA/FSA cards.
- Co-pays are due at the time of service.
- Payment plans are available for balances over $200 — please ask the front
  desk.
